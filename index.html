ABOUT ME

I am a PhD Research Scholar at Rabindranath Tagore University, Assam, currently working on the ecology and taxonomy of termites in Assam, India, under the supervision of Prof. Rezina Ahmed. I completed my Master’s degree in Zoology from Gauhati University, Assam, in 2023.

My research interests broadly focus on the biological diversity of insects in the North Eastern region of India. In addition to my doctoral research on termites, I have also gained field experience in butterfly diversity studies and ethnoentomology, which has strengthened my interest in insect biodiversity and conservation.

RESEARCH

Broad Domain: Entomology

Research Interests:
Ecology and taxonomy of insects, termite diversity and functional ecology, ecosystem processes, insect biodiversity, Ethno-entomological practices in North- East India, Pest-Management Strategies in Paddy Crop.

Current PhD Work:
Ecology and taxonomy of termites in different landscapes of Assam, India, integrating field-based ecological surveys and taxonomic studies.
